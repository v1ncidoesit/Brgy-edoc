Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how the Barangay e-Document Request System (“we”, “our”, “us”) collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use this web application. We value your privacy and are committed to ensuring your personal data is protected and handled responsibly.

1. Information We Collect

  • Full name, address, and contact number
  • Email and password (encrypted)
  • Birth details and parent names (if applicable)
  • Type and purpose of document requests
  • IP address and browser details for security

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to manage your account, process document requests, send updates, and improve system services. We do not sell or share any user information to third parties unless required by law.

3. Data Protection

All data is securely stored in a MySQL database with encryption and limited admin access. We continuously monitor for any unauthorized use or security issues.

4. Your Rights

You may access, update, or delete your information by contacting the barangay administrator through the provided contact channels.
